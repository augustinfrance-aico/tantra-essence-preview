1973 Path begins
Meet Ma Ananda Sarita

My mission is to spread
the joy of Tantra in the world.

My personal path of Tantra began in 1973, when I first stepped onto the journey of awakening.

I had the immense privilege to learn directly from the enlightened mystic Osho for 17 invaluable years. That time allowed me to absorb his wisdom and dive deeper into the realms of meditation, love, and Tantric awareness.

Since 1989, I have committed myself to practising and teaching authentic Tantra, rooted in Meditation and Love.

"Tantra is a sublime path to enlightenment which explores polarities and the alchemical transformation that takes place when opposites meet in the light of awareness."
