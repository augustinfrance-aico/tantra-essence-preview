What this retreat is

Eight days held by Sarita and Modestas - the most-loved sacred touch retreat, offered twice yearly across Bali and Greece. Each day works one chakra through specific massage protocols, meditation, and ritual.

By day eight, the body is mapped - and the touch you give and receive is forever changed. A complete framework you take home: how to listen to a body without rushing, how to hold without grasping, how to give without losing yourself.

Common questions

FAQ

Is this only for couples?

No. Open to everyone - singles, couples, beginners. You will be paired in rotating practice with consent and care.

What if I am uncomfortable with touch?

You set the boundaries. Every practice has a clear consent framework. You can opt out of any session and observe.

What is the difference between Bali and Greece editions?

Same lineage, same curriculum, different lead facilitators. Pick the one that fits your travel.

Can I bring my partner?

Yes - it is encouraged. You will work as a pair and rotate with the group during specific practices.

Who this is for — and who it is not

This is for you if...

  • Anyone curious about Tantra who wants the full chakra map
  • Bodyworkers, massage therapists adding Tantric foundations
  • Couples who want a shared practice they can take home
  • Singles open to consent-based rotating practice
  • Beginners welcome — no prior Tantra experience required

This may not be for you if...

  • Anyone uncomfortable with semi-clothed bodywork
  • People with active back/neck injury (consult our team first)
  • Anyone expecting a luxury spa retreat — this is practice
  • First-trimester pregnancy or post-surgery recovery
  • Those needing a "quick-fix" — this is foundational, not symptomatic
A felt narrative

Eight days, seven chakras, one body

Each day in Bali, we work one chakra. Day one is the root — survival, belonging, the right to take up space. We start there because nothing else opens cleanly until the foundation is felt.

Day two is the sacrum, the waters of life. Day three is the solar plexus, where boundary and warmth meet. Each day a teaching, a massage protocol, then hands-on practice in pairs. Lunch is silent. Afternoons are practice. Evenings are integration in circle.

By day six (heart), the body has become legible to itself. By day seven (throat), couples and singles begin to speak truths they had not even named yet. Day eight closes with the crown chakra and a lineage transmission you carry home.

You leave with a complete framework: how to listen to a body without rushing, how to hold without grasping, how to give without losing yourself. The hands you arrived with are not the hands you leave with.
