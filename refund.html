Refund Policy

Last updated: 2 May 2026

1. Our principle

We want every student to be supported on the path. If something is not the right fit, we will work with you in good faith to find a fair outcome.

2. Online programs

All online programs come with a 30-day money-back guarantee from the date of purchase. If within 30 days you decide the program is not right for you, email us through the contact page and we will refund your purchase in full.

Refunds are processed back to the original payment method within 7-10 business days after our confirmation.

3. Retreats

Retreat deposits are non-refundable but transferable to another retreat in the same calendar year, subject to availability.

Final retreat payment refund schedule:

  • More than 60 days before the retreat: 90% refund
  • 30 to 60 days before: 50% refund
  • Less than 30 days before: no refund, but the spot can be transferred to another person at our discretion

If we cancel a retreat for reasons within our control, you receive a full refund.

4. Force majeure

If a retreat is cancelled or postponed due to circumstances beyond our reasonable control (natural disasters, government restrictions, transport disruptions), we will offer a credit valid for 24 months toward another retreat. Cash refunds are at our discretion in such cases.

5. 1:1 sessions and coaching

1:1 sessions can be rescheduled with at least 48 hours notice. Sessions cancelled with less than 48 hours notice are forfeited. Pre-paid coaching packages are non-refundable but transferable to another time slot within 12 months.

6. Membership and recurring billing

Memberships can be cancelled at any time from your account dashboard. The cancellation takes effect at the end of the current billing period. We do not pro-rate partial months.

7. How to request a refund

Email us through the contact page with: your full name, the email used at purchase, the order number if you have it, and a brief reason. We acknowledge within 48 hours and resolve within 7 business days.

8. Disputes

If we cannot reach an agreement, you may pursue a chargeback with your card issuer. We will cooperate fully with any legitimate inquiry.
