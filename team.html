A felt narrative

How a teacher becomes a teacher in this lineage

Becoming a certified teacher in Sarita's lineage is a minimum two-year path. It begins with the Teacher Training Part 1 (32 days in Bali), which itself is open only to students who already have at least two years of personal Tantra and meditation practice.

After Part 1, the candidate enters a year of supervised teaching practice. They co-lead small workshops alongside senior teachers, receive feedback on every session, and submit recorded teachings for review. Most candidates teach 30-50 hours minimum during this year.

After the year of practice, the candidate joins Teacher Training Part 2 (37 days). This second part deepens the philosophical work, refines holding-space skills, and culminates in a transmission ceremony where the candidate is recognized as a full lineage holder.

Certification is not a credential to add to a marketing bio. It is a recognition that you are now responsible for the lineage in the territory where you teach. Certified teachers are required to maintain an annual continuing-practice with Sarita or a senior teacher, and to abide by the lineage's ethical code — which is stricter than most professional codes you will encounter.

Common questions

FAQ

How long does the full certification take?

Minimum two years from Part 1 to certification. Most candidates take 2.5-3 years — the supervised teaching year is rarely rushed.

Can I teach Tantra without certification?

You can teach what you know. But to teach in this lineage, with our materials and under our name, certification is required. We protect the lineage to protect students.

Is there ongoing supervision after certification?

Yes. Annual continuing practice with Sarita or a senior. Six-monthly check-ins with the lineage council. Public ethical complaints, if any, are reviewed transparently.

Can men become certified teachers?

Yes. The Teacher Training is open to both genders. The Goddess Essence Teacher Training (women's lineage) is women-only by design.

What does it cost?

Part 1: $8,400. Year of supervised practice: free of additional fee, you teach and receive feedback. Part 2: similar to Part 1. Total investment is significant — matching the responsibility you take on.
