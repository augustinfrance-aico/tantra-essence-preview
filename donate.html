Where each contribution actually goes

Tantra Essence is not a non-profit. It is a small company built around a teaching, with a team of teachers and operations staff. But part of what we do depends on contributions — and that part has been growing.

The free masterclasses we offer publicly cost real money to host, edit, and translate. The scholarship fund that brings 8-12 students per year onto retreats they could not otherwise afford is funded entirely by contributions. The Mt Kailash pilgrimage subsidy for participants from countries with currency disadvantages is contribution-funded.

Last year, contributions covered 23,000 free-class views, 11 retreat scholarships, and the translation of three foundational masterclasses into Spanish and Portuguese. The money goes to the work, not to overhead.

If you give, you receive an emailed receipt and an annual update on what your contribution made possible. You will not be added to a separate marketing list. Your name is never published unless you specifically ask to be acknowledged.

Common questions

FAQ

Is my contribution tax-deductible?

No. Tantra Essence LLC is a Wyoming-registered LLC, not a 501(c)(3). Contributions are gifts, not tax-deductible donations.

Can I sponsor a specific student or retreat?

Yes. Mention it in the contact form after your contribution and we will direct it to the scholarship fund earmarked for the closest match.

Can I set up a recurring contribution?

Yes — in the contribution form, choose "monthly" instead of "one-time". You can cancel anytime from your account dashboard.

How transparent is the spending?

Annually, we send contributors a one-page summary of how the money was used. We will not pretend to be a non-profit but we will be honest about where each pound or dollar went.

Can I contribute in something other than money?

Yes. Volunteer time, translation, photography, retreat space, sound equipment — reach us through the contact form with your offering.
