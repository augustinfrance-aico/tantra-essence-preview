Frequently asked

Questions seekers often have

I'm completely new to Tantra. Where do I start?
Start with the free "Hidden Science of Tantric Awakening" masterclass — 90 minutes that orients you to the whole path. From there, if you're a woman: Goddess Unveiled. If you're a man: Master Lover. If you're a couple: Sacred Union. All are designed for absolute beginners.
Is this safe? I've heard mixed things about Tantra online.
Tantra Essence is a 100% non-sexual, fully clothed practice. Sarita carries 53 years of lineage and is internationally recognized as the most respected female Tantra teacher alive. Every program is trauma-aware and consent-based.
How much time per week do I need?
Programs are self-paced. Most students complete a 12-module program in 6-12 weeks at 2-3 hours per week. You have lifetime access — go at the pace your life allows.
Can I do this if I'm single? In a relationship?
Both. About 60% of our students start solo. Programs like Goddess Unveiled, Master Lover, and the meditations are designed for individual practice. Sacred Union and Couples Retreats are for partnerships.
Do I get a certificate?
Online programs include a certificate of completion. Teacher Trainings (Bali, Goddess Essence) provide internationally recognized teacher certifications backed by Tantra Essence's 25-year reputation.
What if I can't afford the full price?
Most programs offer 3-month payment plans. Membership is €39/mo (less than a yoga class). Plus 50+ free meditations and 3 free masterclasses to begin without paying anything.
Can I cancel?
Anytime. Cancel membership in 2 clicks from your dashboard. Programs come with a 14-day full refund guarantee. We never lock you in.
