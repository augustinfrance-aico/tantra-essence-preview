How your library actually works

When you purchase a program, your access is permanent. Lifetime means lifetime — for as long as the platform exists, you can return to it. We have students from 2014 who still log in and rewatch lessons. The platform has changed providers twice. Their access has always carried over.

The library tracks your progress automatically. Where you stopped, what you completed, when you last accessed each lesson. This is not used for marketing — it is shown to you so you can pick up where you left off. We do not send "you haven't logged in lately!" guilt-trip emails.

For programs with live components (cohort programs, live Q&A), recordings are added to your library within 48 hours of each session. You can rewatch any time. Live calls are transcribed for accessibility.

You can download audio meditations for offline use. Video lessons stream only (to keep the lifetime access manageable on our side). Files for printable practice cards are downloadable at any time.

Common questions

FAQ

What if I lose access to my email?

Email us through the contact form with your full name and approximate date of purchase. We can verify and restore access via a new email.

Can I download videos for offline?

Audio yes, video no — for licensing reasons. The platform is reliable for streaming on slow connections.

What about cohort program recordings?

Added within 48 hours of each live session. Yours forever in your library.

Can I share my account with my partner?

For couples programs (like Sacred Touch), yes — both partners can use the same login. For individual programs, please respect the personal license.

What happens to my library if I cancel a membership?

Memberships and one-time purchases are different. One-time program purchases stay in your library forever. Membership content disappears at the end of the membership period.
