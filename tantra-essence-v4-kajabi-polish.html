Hi! I'm Ma Ananda Sarita

Disciple of Osho. Teacher of Tantra. Lover of life.

My mission is to spread the joy of Tantra in the world. I have been deeply immersed in my personal path of Tantra for over 53 years, with 17 years living and learning at the side of Osho himself.

Today I guide thousands of seekers worldwide — through online programs, sacred retreats in Bali, Switzerland, India, and the United States, and through the Kaula community of certified teachers in 36 countries.
