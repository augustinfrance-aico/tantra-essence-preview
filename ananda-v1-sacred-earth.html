About Ma Ananda Sarita

Three decades of sacred transmission

Ma Ananda Sarita is a direct disciple of Osho and one of the world's most respected Tantra masters. For over 30 years she has dedicated her life to awakening the sacred essence in men, women, and couples across the globe.

Her teachings blend the ancient wisdom of classical Tantra with the living presence of a master who walks the path daily. Her work has touched tens of thousands of students through retreats, trainings, and transmissions in more than 40 countries.

Common Questions

Answers for curious seekers

Is Tantra about sexuality?
Tantra is a complete spiritual path that embraces all of life, including sexuality — but it is far more than that. It is a path of expanding awareness, deepening presence, and meeting the sacred in every breath. Sexuality, when present, is one of many doorways.
I'm completely new to Tantra. Where do I start?
The free masterclass is the perfect entry point. From there, the Tantra Essentials Masterclass gives you a complete foundation. Many students then choose a path for Women, Men, or Couples based on where their heart calls them.
Do I need a partner to practice?
No. Most of our programs are designed for individuals. The Couples path is specifically for partners who wish to journey together, but the vast majority of the work happens in solo practice — the relationship to yourself is always the foundation.
What is included in the online library?
Members receive unlimited access to 200+ guided meditations, 80+ sacred music tracks, 150+ video transmissions, and a growing archive of Ma Ananda Sarita's teachings. New content is added every month.
How do retreats work?
Retreats are held in sacred locations around the world — Bali, India, Costa Rica, Europe. Each retreat is an immersion of 7 to 21 days with daily practice, ceremony, and direct transmission. Apply early as spaces are limited.
Is the teacher training right for me?
The Goddess Essence Teacher Training is a 12-month journey for women who feel called to hold Tantra space for others. It includes online modules, live cohort calls, and a final in-person retreat. Admission is by application.
