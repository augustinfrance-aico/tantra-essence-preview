A felt narrative

How we choose what to sell

The Tantra Essence shop is small on purpose. We sell what we use, what students keep coming back for, and what we can stand behind. Six categories of products. None of them are best-sellers in any meaningful sense — we sell maybe a few hundred pieces a year of each, and that is fine.

The Tachyon items are sourced from a single producer we have worked with since 2014. They are charged in a specific way that the producer has refined over twenty-five years. We do not pretend to know whether Tachyon "works" the way the producer claims — we know that students who use them report a felt difference, and that is enough for us to keep stocking them.

The music is recorded by Sarita and senior students in actual ceremonial settings. Not in a studio. The slight imperfections are intentional — you are not buying production polish, you are buying the thing that was sung in the room. The books are written by Sarita over forty years of teaching. They are dense.

Shipping is handled from two warehouses (US and Europe). Delivery is typically 5-12 business days depending on country. Music and book downloads are immediate after purchase.

Common questions

FAQ

Are the Tachyon items "real" Tachyon?

We rely on the producer's certification. We do not run independent scientific tests. Use them based on your own felt experience, not on our claims.

Can I return a product?

Tachyon items: returnable within 14 days if unworn and in original packaging. Music and books: digital purchases are non-refundable but we will replace damaged physical items.

Do you ship internationally?

Yes — from our two warehouses (US and EU). Customs duties, where applicable, are the buyer's responsibility.

Are the books available in other languages?

Three of Sarita's books are translated into Spanish, two into Portuguese, one into French and German. Translations are noted on each book's page.

Can I buy in bulk for a yoga studio or workshop?

Yes — please contact us through the support page for wholesale pricing on quantities of 12+.
