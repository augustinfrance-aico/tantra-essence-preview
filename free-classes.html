A felt narrative

Why we actually give classes away

There is a marketing wisdom that says: give away the foundation, charge for the depth. We agree, but only halfway. The free classes we offer are not stripped-down teasers — they are real teachings that stand on their own.

The Free Tantra Masterclass with Sarita is sixty minutes of taught content. By the end of it, you have a working definition of Tantra, a feel for the chakra map, and a 15-minute daily ritual. If you never bought another thing from us, you would have a practice you could keep for life. That is intentional.

The same holds for the Womb Wisdom audio, the Morning Light Activation, the Sacred Touch Preview. Each one is a complete piece. You can use them as long as you want without ever joining a paid program.

Most students who join paid programs do so months or years after their first free class. They watch the masterclass, do the morning ritual, sit on it, and only when they feel ready do they take a next step. That slow path is the real path. We design our offerings to support it rather than rush it.

Common questions

FAQ

Do I have to give my email to access free classes?

Yes. We need a way to send you the access link. You can unsubscribe from the newsletter in one click after receiving it.

Will I be added to a long sales sequence?

You will receive 3-5 welcome emails over two weeks introducing different aspects of the lineage. Then you join the standard weekly newsletter (3 minutes per week). Unsubscribe anytime.

Can I share the free classes with friends?

We prefer you send them the signup link rather than the content directly. The signup is free for them too.

Are the free classes the same as paid programs?

They are foundational. Paid programs go deeper, include practice cards, live circles, community access, and supervised instruction. The free classes are not "lite" — they are different in scope.

Why is it free?

Because the foundation should be accessible. We charge for depth, retreats, and certification — not for the doorway.
