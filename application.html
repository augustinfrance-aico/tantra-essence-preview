Why this is not a generic form

The Tantra Transformation Application exists because we made a decision early on: we would rather have fewer students on the path than push the wrong students through. The application form is the gate.

Each application is read in full by a senior teacher. We do not skim. We look at where you are, what you wrote about what is calling you, what kind of container would suit you, and whether the retreat or program you are considering is actually the right fit.

About 40% of applications are gently redirected — not rejected, but pointed toward a different offering. A complete beginner applying for Vigyan Bhairav usually receives a recommendation to start with Tantra Essentials and Sacred Touch first. An advanced practitioner applying for the Free Masterclass receives a personal note suggesting the Teacher Training instead.

Replies typically come within seven days. They are not automated. They are not formulaic. They are written by someone who has read what you wrote and has a felt sense of what would serve you.

Common questions

FAQ

What if I am not ready for the retreat I picked?

We will tell you honestly and suggest where to start. Most people appreciate this rather than the alternative of arriving at a retreat that will not serve them.

Will my information stay private?

Yes. Your application is read by a senior teacher and stored privately. It is never used for marketing. After 12 months, if you have not become a student, it is deleted.

Can I apply for multiple retreats at once?

Yes — mention them in the form. We will reply with our recommended sequence based on your stated path stage.

Is there an application fee?

No. The application is free. We charge for the retreat, not for considering you.

What if my application is declined?

We rarely decline outright. We redirect. If we genuinely feel a retreat is not safe or appropriate (very rare), we will explain clearly and suggest preparation steps.
