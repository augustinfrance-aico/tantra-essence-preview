A felt narrative

Six weeks, twenty-four lessons, one couple at home

Week one builds the foundation. Three video lessons (around 25-30 min each), one printable practice card, and a weekly live circle. You are not asked to do anything dramatic in week one — just to sit together and breathe with awareness.

Week two enters the chakra map. You learn how to find your own root, sacrum, womb / hara, heart, throat, third eye, crown. You begin to map your partner. The exercises are gentle. Most couples report week two as the week sleep got deeper.

Weeks three and four teach the foundational sacred touch protocols. Twelve strokes that you will practice for years. By week four, you are giving each other 30-minute sessions and most couples are quietly stunned by what is now possible at home.

Weeks five and six unfold tantric union and integration. The practices are taught with consent and care, in your own home, on your own pace. You finish with a practice you keep for life and a community of Sacred Touch couples around the world.
