Questions

You're not alone in wondering

Do I need any prior experience with Tantra?

None at all. Goddess Unveiled is designed for absolute beginners as well as experienced practitioners. Sarita guides you from the foundations.

How long do I have access to the program?

Lifetime access. Once you join, the program is yours forever — including all future updates and bonus content Sarita adds.

How much time per week should I commit?

2 to 3 hours per week is the sweet spot. The program is self-paced, so you can adjust to your life rhythm. Most women complete it in 4 to 6 months.

Is this safe for women with trauma history?

Yes, and we approach this with great care. The practices are gentle, trauma-informed, and you are always in choice. We also recommend pairing with a therapist if needed.

What if it doesn't resonate with me?

We offer a 14-day money-back guarantee. Try the first 2 modules, and if it's not for you, write to us — we'll refund you, no questions asked.

Is the program in English?

Yes. All teachings are in English, with subtitles available. Multi-language versions (Spanish, Italian) are coming soon.
