The Path

A life dedicated to Tantra

I have been deeply immersed in my personal path of Tantra, Meditation and Spirituality since 1973. During my work as healer and coach, I observed that the root cause for many physical, emotional and mental problems in people stems from unresolved sexual and relationship issues.

Tantra is the perfect antidote for these issues. Tantra offers transformation on all levels, accepting the human being as he or she is and using this as a door into expansion of consciousness.

"The more I practiced Tantra methods alone or in love union, the more I felt compelled to share these divine experiences with people everywhere in the world."

There is a global awakening happening right now, for personal and planetary enlightenment. Tantra offers a wise path for this to manifest.

The Essence

The six pillars of Tantra

My approach is committed to authentic Tantra, rooted in Meditation and Love — a sublime path to enlightenment exploring polarities and the alchemical transformation when opposites meet in the light of awareness.

i

Meditation

Methods for expansion of consciousness — the foundation of every authentic awakening.

ii

Devotional Love

Melting the ego by putting love above all else. The heart leads, the mind follows.

iii

Beauty

Aesthetic sensitivity nurtures a state of gratitude. Beauty is a doorway to the sacred.

iv

Rituals

Conscious alignment with the powers of creation. Sacred gestures, sacred timing.

v

Joy

Joy is the natural state of the awakened heart. Tantra cultivates joy as practice.

vi

Laughter

Realising the cosmic joke brings a lightness of being. Awakening doesn't have to be serious.

Lineage

17 years learning directly with Osho

I spent 17 years learning directly from the Enlightened Mystic Osho, in his physical presence and a total of 26 years in his community.

I was blessed beyond measure to absorb his powerful transmission of meditation and Tantra in multidimensional ways. Even after he left his body in 1990, his mystic presence continues to ignite a fire of transformation in countless people around the world.

"I am honoured to offer what I have learned with him as a continuity of his lineage."

Life Journey

A timeline of awakening

From a 9-year-old asking "what is the essence of life?" to teaching across 5 continents.

Early Childhood

The first question

As a young child, I started contemplating the key question — "What is the essence of life?" At age 9, I had my first spiritual awakening experience.

Age 15 — 1968

Love for dance

I fell in love with dance and sensed the essence of life must be based in movement. I travelled to New York where I won a modern dance scholarship and studied with the Joffrey Ballet school.

Age 16 — 1969

Hitchhiked the world

I hitchhiked throughout North America, Europe and the Middle East, finally reaching India in late 1972.

1973

Met Osho in Mumbai

I met Osho in Mumbai in 1973 at his discourse on the Vigyan Bhairav Tantra. He was teaching a sutra I still remember by heart — "While engaged in sexual union stay attentive on the fire in the beginning and so continuing, avoid the embers in the end."

1974 – 2000

26 years in Osho's community

I lived in Osho's community for 26 years, receiving much personal guidance from him on Meditation, Tantra, Love and Relationships.

1975 – 1983

Work as meditation

I cleaned Osho's own home for 8 years, learning that outer cleaning is a representation of inner purification.

1982 – 1989

Press & Public Relations

I worked in the Public and Press Relations Department in Osho's community, a position I held for 7 years.

1990

Colorpuncture & Osho Multiversity

I became Director of the Colorpuncture department in the Healing Arts wing of Osho Multiversity. I taught a 2-month advanced Colorpuncture training and offered numerous individual healing sessions.

1999

School of Awakening, Europe

I started a School in Europe devoted to Tantra and Healing Arts named School of Awakening.

2000s

Author & recordings

I published two books on Tantra named "Tantric Love" and "Divine Sexuality" and created numerous Tantra meditations and CDs.

2008

Tantra Essence is born

I started Tantra Essence in 2008 and began publishing a monthly newsletter.

2008 — today

International retreats

I began teaching internationally. To date I have taught numerous retreats and trainings in 5 continents and 30+ countries.

Today — Bali

Teaching from Bali

I am currently based in Bali where I teach several live retreats and trainings throughout the year. I also created several online programs for those who cannot travel all the way to Bali.
