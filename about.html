Her path

Ma Ananda Sarita's mission is to spread the joy of Tantra in the world. She has been deeply immersed in her personal path of Tantra, meditation, and spirituality since 1973, with 17 years living and learning directly at the side of the enlightened mystic Osho.

Sarita is committed to practicing and teaching authentic Tantra, rooted in meditation and love. She views Tantra as a sublime path to enlightenment which explores polarities and the alchemical transformation that takes place when opposites meet in the light of awareness.

"Tantra is not what most people think. It is the most ancient, most embodied, and most direct path of awakening. After 53 years walking this path, I'm here to give it to you — without dilution, without dogma, with all my love."

Today she guides thousands of seekers worldwide — through online programs, sacred retreats in Bali, Switzerland, India, and the United States, and through the Kaula community of 500+ certified teachers in 36 countries.
