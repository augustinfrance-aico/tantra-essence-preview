Overview

What this retreat is

Master Lover is the most-loved couples container in Sarita's catalog. Seven Bali days held by Ma Ananda Sarita and Modestas - open to singles and couples ready for the deepest field of intimacy this lineage offers.

From breath to touch to the alchemy of two becoming one, the retreat unfolds the slow, precise, eternal art of love as a spiritual practice. Held with consent and care, in private rooms and in held container as practice deepens.

Common questions

FAQ

Do I need a partner to attend?

No. Master Lover welcomes both singles and couples. The practices are taught in pairs but partners are sometimes shifted within the group with mutual consent.

What level of experience is required?

Open to all levels but a meditation or yoga practice is recommended. If brand new to Tantra, we suggest the Free Masterclass first.

What is the application process?

Submit the application form. A senior facilitator reads each one and replies within 7 days.

Are flights included?

No. Flights to Denpasar (DPS) are not included. We send arrival/departure logistics and recommended hotels for the night before.

Honest fit check

Who this is for — and who it is not

This is for you if...

  • Couples in their first 5 years together who want a strong foundation
  • Long-term couples (10+ years) who feel the drift and want to come back
  • Couples after a difficult passage (illness, loss, infidelity) ready to repair
  • Singles open to deep partner work with rotating practice (with consent)
  • Both partners able to commit to 7 days fully present, no phones

This may not be for you if...

  • Couples in active crisis — this is not couples therapy
  • Anyone uncomfortable with consent-based touch protocols
  • People expecting "spiritual sexual healing" as therapy substitute
  • Anyone who needs a vacation rather than a practice container
  • Pregnant women in first trimester (medical caution)
A felt narrative

How a week with us actually unfolds

You arrive on day one carrying everything — the small fights, the unsaid things, the years where life took up the space love used to. We do not ask you to leave it at the door. We ask you to bring it forward and let it be seen.

By day three, the body has slowed. Sleep deepens. The first eye-gazing session lands. Couples begin to actually meet again, sometimes for the first time in years. There are tears here. There is also laughter that surprises you because it had been gone.

Days four to six unfold the deeper practices. Sacred touch becomes a language you both know. Tantric union is taught with consent and care, in private rooms when you are ready. The retreat is held by Sarita and Modestas with thirty plus years of experience between them — nothing is rushed, nothing is forced.

Day seven closes with a beloved ceremony. You renew your vows in front of the group, or in private if you prefer. Most couples cry. Most couples laugh. Most couples leave holding each other in a way they had forgotten was possible.
